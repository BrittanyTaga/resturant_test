Resturants around Litchfield Park, AZ

Here in Litchfield Park, there are a couple of resturants that I personally enjoy.

  • Teabo Coffee & Sandwhiches

    Teabo Coffee & Sandwhiches was first found due to the boba drinks I was trying to find.

  • NYPD Pizza

    The pizza here is huge and can easily fill my stomach for a week.

  • Islands Restuarant Avandale

    Islands food is a local resturant chain that I grew up with. It is a Hawaiin style burger joint with a sports bar.

  • Yogis Grill - Avondale

    Have not yet visited but the food presentation looks good and the reviews are good as well.

  • Hayashi Hibachi

    I have yet to try this resturant as well but I do like the selection. With this selection, it is easier to avoid allergies. From September 1, 2021, the company has yet to update their website with the Arizona location.
